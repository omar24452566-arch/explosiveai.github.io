Yes, You Can Make Nano Banana Videos

A "nano banana video" is a 5-second product promo for the world's tiniest fruit brand. Here's exactly how to make one with AI tools in under 10 minutes — for about $0.12.

Click to see the step-by-step workflow ↓

Let's say you run NanoBanana™ — a startup selling miniature bananas. Here's your complete AI video pipeline, step by step:

01
Write the Script
Open ChatGPT. Prompt: "Write a 5-second product hook script for NanoBanana™ — the world's smallest banana. Punchy, funny, memorable." Cost: Free.
02
Generate the Video
Take your script to Sora. Prompt: "Cinematic close-up of a tiny banana on a marble surface, golden light, slow dolly-in." Takes 90 seconds.
03
Add Your Voice
Paste your script into HeyGen. Pick a presenter avatar or clone your own voice. Sync it to the Sora clip. Cost: ~$0.12 per minute.
04
Repurpose to All Formats
Drop the finished 5-second video into Opus Clip. Get it auto-resized for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and LinkedIn simultaneously.
05
Write the Captions
Back to Copy.ai: "Write 5 viral social captions for a NanoBanana™ promo video." Post to all platforms. Your nano banana empire begins.

Total time: 12 minutes  ·  Total cost: ~$0.50  ·  Platforms reached: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn  ·  Banana empire status: Initiated 🍌
